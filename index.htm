Welcome to Blevins Harps! Our goal is to inform and educate you so that you can make the best possible decision regarding your harp purchase. Blevins Instruments, Inc. is owned and operated by Dwight Blevins and Cindy Kleinstuber Blevins. We are well into our 3rd decade of harp building here in beautiful Western Colorado. We have a harp to suit every player and nearly every budget!
