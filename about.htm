ABOUT US

About Us

Blevins Instruments, Inc. is owned and operated by Dwight Blevins and Cindy Kleinstuber Blevins. We are well into our 3rd decade of harp building here in beautiful Western Colorado.

Cindy Kleinstuber Blevins is known world-wide for the original harp music she writes. Her large and diverse body of work includes harp solos (lap harp to pedal harp, music for every harpist and every harp playing event), learn-to-play materials, ensembles and commissioned work.

Our Harps

Our handcrafted harps are all lever harps (also called folk harps, or Celtic harps). You can choose your lever type and number of levers upon order. We price most of our harps with full levers. However, we can price out any lever configuration you wish. Floor harps are generally sold with full levers, but you can ask us to price out "less levers" if you wish.
